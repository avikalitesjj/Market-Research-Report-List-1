<p><p>The Non-Clog Pumps Market features two primary types: Vortex Type and Impeller Type. Vortex Type pumps utilize a radial flow design that creates a vortex to handle solids and debris without clogging, making them suitable for wastewater and sewage applications. In contrast, Impeller Type pumps have specialized impellers designed to move fluids with suspended solids while minimizing blockages. Both types are essential for efficient fluid transport in sectors such as municipal, industrial, and agricultural applications where clogging is a concern.</p></p>
