<p><ul><li>Cable Driver</li><li>Chain Driver</li><li>Traction (MRL) Elevator</li><li>Hydraulic Elevator</li><li>Pneumatic Lift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home elevator market features several types: </p><p>1. **Cable-Driven Elevators** use steel cables and pulleys for smooth vertical transport.</p><p>2. **Chain-Driven Elevators** leverage a chain mechanism for efficient movement, typically suited for heavier loads.</p><p>3. **Traction (MRL) Elevators** employ a machine-room-less design, providing space-saving benefits without sacrificing performance.</p><p>4. **Hydraulic Elevators** rely on fluid pressure for lifting, ideal for low-rise buildings with a robust construction.</p><p>5. **Pneumatic Lifts** utilize air pressure, offering a compact and eco-friendly alternative for residential use.</p></p>
