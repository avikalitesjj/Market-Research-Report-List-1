<p><p>The cortical electrode market comprises various types, primarily strip and grid electrodes. Strip electrodes are linear, flexible devices used for monitoring and recording electrical activity from specific brain regions, allowing targeted applications in epilepsy treatment and brain-computer interfaces. Grid electrodes, with a matrix configuration, enable comprehensive mapping of larger brain areas, providing detailed information on brain function and aiding in surgical planning. Both types are crucial for advancing neurodiagnostic techniques and enhancing our understanding of brain disorders.</p></p>
