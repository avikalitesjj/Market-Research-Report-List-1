<p><strong>The Industrial Inkjet Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIJ</li><li>DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printer market primarily consists of two types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-On-Demand (DOD) printers. CIJ technology continuously circulates ink, allowing for high-speed printing on various substrates, making it suitable for large-scale manufacturing and coding applications. In contrast, DOD printers release ink droplets only when needed, which enhances precision and is ideal for high-quality graphics and variable data printing. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing productivity and quality in marking and packaging processes.</p></p>
