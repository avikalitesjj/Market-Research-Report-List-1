<p><p>The ceramic membrane equipment market encompasses three primary types: microfiltration, ultrafiltration, and composite membranes. Microfiltration membranes effectively remove larger particles and sediments, making them suitable for water treatment and food processing. Ultrafiltration membranes target smaller solutes, including proteins and viruses, providing enhanced separation for wastewater treatment and pharmaceutical applications. Composite membranes combine different materials to optimize performance, offering improved permeability and chemical resistance, thus catering to diverse industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental management.</p></p>
