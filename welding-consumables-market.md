<p><p>The welding consumables market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Stick electrodes are versatile and effective for manual welding processes. Solid wires are commonly used in MIG welding, providing strong, consistent welds. Flux-cored wires offer increased productivity and are ideal for outdoor use, as they contain flux that protects the weld. SAW (Submerged Arc Welding) wires and fluxes are designed for high-efficiency industrial applications, delivering deep penetration and high-quality welds in heavy-duty projects.</p></p>
