<p><p>The diagnostic/medical imaging market encompasses various technologies essential for non-invasive diagnosis and treatment monitoring. X-ray imaging systems utilize radiation to visualize internal structures, while computed tomography (CT) scanners produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to create real-time images, often during pregnancy. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) systems employ magnetic fields and radio waves for high-resolution images of soft tissues. Nuclear imaging systems use radioactive materials to visualize physiological functions and diagnose diseases.</p></p>
