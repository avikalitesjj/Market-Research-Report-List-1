<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PCR PET Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>m-PCR</li><li>c-PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR PET resin market is divided into two main types: m-PCR and c-PCR. m-PCR (molecular PCR) involves recycling post-consumer PET waste into high-quality resin suitable for various applications, maintaining performance similar to virgin PET. c-PCR (chemical PCR), on the other hand, is derived from chemically recycling PET, breaking it down to its original monomers and allowing for the creation of new PET resin with a potentially lower environmental impact. Both types contribute to sustainable practices in the plastics industry.</p></p>
