<p><p>The disposable medical cotton ball market comprises two main types: sterile and non-sterile medical cotton balls. Sterile medical cotton balls are manufactured under strict hygienic conditions, making them suitable for direct contact with open wounds and for surgical procedures, thus minimizing infection risks. Both types are essential in various healthcare and personal care settings.</p></p>
