<p><p>The hydraulic rotary unions market primarily comprises two types: multi-passage and single-passage unions. Multi-passage rotary unions facilitate the transfer of multiple hydraulic lines and fluids within the same unit, allowing for efficient operation in complex systems. In contrast, single-passage rotary unions are designed for simple, straightforward applications, transmitting a single fluid line with minimal complexities. Both types are essential in various industries, enhancing performance and reliability in equipment where rotating movement and fluid transfer are required.</p></p>
