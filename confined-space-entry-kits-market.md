<p><p>Confined Space Entry Kits are specialized equipment designed for safety in hazardous environments. Vertical Entry Kits facilitate access through vertical openings, often used in manholes or tanks, equipped with gear for hoisting and descending. Side Entry Kits, conversely, allow entry through horizontal openings like ducts or storage tanks, featuring tools and harnesses tailored for lateral access. Both kit types prioritize safety, ensuring proper ventilation, communication, and emergency protocols to protect workers during confined space operations.</p></p>
