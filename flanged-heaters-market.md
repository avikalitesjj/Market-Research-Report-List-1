<p><p>Flanged heaters are integral in industrial heating applications, categorized into straight flange and L-shaped flange types. Straight flange heaters feature a flat, straight design optimized for uniform heat distribution, making them ideal for straightforward installations. Conversely, L-shaped flange heaters offer an angled configuration, accommodating space constraints or specific heating requirements in tight environments. Both types ensure efficient thermal transfer and are widely used in processes such as heating liquids, gases, and solids across various industries like chemical, food, and manufacturing.</p></p>
