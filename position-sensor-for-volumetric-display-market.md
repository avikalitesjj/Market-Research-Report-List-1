<p><p>Position sensors for volumetric displays utilize technologies like Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS). DLP employs micro-mirrors to manipulate light, creating dynamic 3D visuals. In contrast, LCOS combines liquid crystal displays with silicon backing for high-resolution images, making it ideal for detailed volumetric projections. Both technologies enable precise tracking of viewer positions, ensuring interactive and immersive experiences in applications like gaming, medical imaging, and architectural visualization. Their unique characteristics cater to diverse market demands.</p></p>
