<p><p>Multi-cloud management encompasses three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Public Cloud**: Services provided over the internet, allowing users to access resources on-demand without ownership responsibilities, ideal for scalability and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>2. **Private Cloud**: Exclusive cloud infrastructure dedicated to a single organization, offering enhanced security and control, suitable for sensitive data management.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud**: A combination of public and private clouds, enabling data and application mobility, thus allowing organizations to leverage both environments for flexibility and optimized resource utilization.</p></p>
