<p><p>Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) films are categorized into three primary types: Borazon, Cubonite, and Elbor. Borazon is renowned for its exceptional hardness and thermal stability, making it ideal for abrasive applications. Cubonite combines CBN's properties with unique film characteristics, enhancing performance in cutting tools. Elbor, often used in specialized tools, offers superior wear resistance and is effective at high temperatures. Together, these CBN films cater to diverse industrial needs, particularly in machining and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
