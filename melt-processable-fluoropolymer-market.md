<p><p>The melt processable fluoropolymer market comprises several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) is known for its excellent chemical resistance and electrical insulation. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) offers high mechanical strength and UV resistance. PFA-MFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) combines thermal stability with ease of processing. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) is valued for its durability and chemical resistance. ECTFE (Ethylene Chlorotrifluoroethylene) is recognized for its high purity and corrosion resistance. Other fluoropolymers also contribute to niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
