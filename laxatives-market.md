<p><p>The laxatives market consists of several types, each serving distinct purposes. Hypertonic laxatives draw water into the intestines, softening stools. Lubricating laxatives coat stool, easing its passage. Stimulant laxatives provoke bowel contractions for quicker relief. Volumetric laxatives increase stool bulk, promoting regularity. Swelling laxatives absorb water, expanding within the intestines to stimulate movement. Additionally, the "Others" category includes various agents that don't fit the primary classifications, offering diverse options for constipation relief.</p></p>
