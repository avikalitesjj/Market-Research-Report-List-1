<p><p>Starch recovery systems encompass various technologies designed to enhance starch extraction and purification processes. Refining sieves separate starch from impurities, while hydrocyclones utilize centrifugal force for efficient sedimentation. Centrifuges enhance separation by rapidly spinning mixtures, and vacuum filters aid in liquid removal through porous media. Screw conveyors facilitate the movement of starch materials efficiently, and filling stations ensure proper packaging of final products. Together, these systems optimize starch recovery, improving yield and purity across diverse applications in food and industrial sectors.</p></p>
