<p><p>The electric vehicle market primarily consists of two types: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) and Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs). PHEVs combine a conventional internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing for both electric-only driving and traditional fuel use, appealing to consumers seeking flexibility. BEVs, on the other hand, rely solely on electric energy stored in batteries, offering zero emissions and requiring charging infrastructure. Both types reflect the growing shift towards sustainable transportation and cater to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
