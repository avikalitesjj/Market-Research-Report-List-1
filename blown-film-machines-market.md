<p><p>Blown film machines are categorized based on the number of polymer layers they can produce. Three-layer machines are ideal for applications requiring moderate barrier properties and cost efficiency. Five-layer machines offer enhanced performance, including better mechanical strength and barrier characteristics, suitable for food packaging and industrial uses. Seven-layer machines provide advanced functionality, allowing for specialized properties like high barrier and unique film structures for demanding applications. Other configurations may include custom designs tailored for specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
