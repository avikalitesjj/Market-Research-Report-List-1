<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are critical components in refrigeration and air conditioning systems, regulating refrigerant flow with precision for enhanced efficiency. The market for EEVs is primarily segmented into electromagnetic EEVs and electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs utilize a solenoid mechanism for operation, while electric EEVs employ stepper motors for more accurate control of refrigerant flow. Both types enhance system performance, optimize energy consumption, and enable advanced control strategies, making them essential for modern HVAC applications.</p></p>
