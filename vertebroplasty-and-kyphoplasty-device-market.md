<p><p>Vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty are minimally invasive procedures used to treat vertebral compression fractures, often resulting from osteoporosis or trauma. These procedures involve the insertion of a bone cement mixture into fractured vertebrae to stabilize the spine and alleviate pain. The vertebroplasty device allows for the direct injection of cement, while kyphoplasty also includes the use of an inflatable balloon to restore vertebral height before cement placement.</p><p>The Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as the rising prevalence of osteoporosis, an aging population, and increasing awareness of minimally invasive surgical techniques.
