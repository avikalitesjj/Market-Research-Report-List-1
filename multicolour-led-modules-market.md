<p><p>The multicolour LED modules market is categorized into three types based on power consumption. Low Power modules (0.3W and below) are ideal for small applications requiring minimal energy, such as indicators and decorative lighting. Middle Power modules (0.3-0.5W) offer a balance of brightness and efficiency, suitable for accent lighting and backlighting. High Power modules (1W and above) deliver intense brightness for larger installations, commonly used in architectural lighting, signage, and outdoor displays, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
