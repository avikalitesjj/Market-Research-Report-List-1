<p><ul><li>Double Start</li><li>Single Start</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bucket auger drilling involves two primary market types: double start and single start. Double start bucket augers feature two helical flights, allowing for faster drilling and improved material extraction, making them suitable for large-scale projects where efficiency is essential. In contrast, single start bucket augers have a single helical flight, providing better control and precision for smaller projects or detailed work. Both types cater to different market needs based on project size, speed, and depth requirements in drilling applications.</p></p>
