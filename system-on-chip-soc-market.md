<p><p>A System on Chip (SoC) integrates various components, such as processors, memory, and interfaces, onto a single chip, optimizing space and energy efficiency. The market can be categorized into digital, analog, mixed signal, and others. Digital SoCs primarily process binary data for applications like smartphones. Analog SoCs deal with real-world signals, essential for audio and sensor applications. Mixed signal SoCs combine both functionalities, catering to versatile needs. The "Others" category encompasses specialized applications like automotive and IoT-related solutions.</p></p>
