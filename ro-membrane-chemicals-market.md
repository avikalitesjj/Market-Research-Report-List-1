<p><p>The RO membrane chemicals market encompasses several key types, including antiscalants, cleaning chemicals, and pretreatment chemicals. Antiscalants are used to prevent mineral scaling on membranes, enhancing their lifespan and efficiency. Cleaning chemicals are employed to remove fouling and contaminants from membrane surfaces, ensuring optimal performance. Pretreatment chemicals prepare water before it enters the RO system, improving feed water quality and protecting the membranes. Together, these chemicals play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of reverse osmosis systems.</p></p>
