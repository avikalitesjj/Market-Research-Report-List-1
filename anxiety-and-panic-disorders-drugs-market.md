<p><p>The anxiety and panic disorders drugs market primarily comprises two main types: antidepressant and anxiolytic drugs. Antidepressants, such as SSRIs and SNRIs, are used to treat anxiety disorders by boosting serotonin and norepinephrine levels in the brain, enhancing mood and reducing anxiety symptoms. Anxiolytic drugs, primarily benzodiazepines, provide rapid relief from anxiety by acting on the central nervous system to produce a calming effect. Both drug classes are essential for managing anxiety-related conditions and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
