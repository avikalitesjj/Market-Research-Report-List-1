<p><ul><li>Surface Drainage Gutters</li><li>Internal Drainage Gutters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel gutter market is segmented into two main types: surface drainage gutters and internal drainage gutters. Surface drainage gutters are designed to collect and channel rainwater from roofs and pavements directly into drainage systems, ensuring effective water management and preventing flooding. In contrast, internal drainage gutters are integrated within the structure of buildings, directing rainwater away from critical areas and reducing the risk of water damage. Both types utilize stainless steel for durability, corrosion resistance, and longevity.</p></p>
