<p><strong>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED display market consists mainly of two types: Active Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays utilize a thin-film transistor array to control individual pixels, allowing for higher resolution, faster refresh rates, and better power efficiency, making them ideal for smartphones and TVs. In contrast, PMOLED displays use a simpler structure with direct control over rows and columns, resulting in lower power consumption but limited to smaller screens and less demanding applications like wearables or simple displays.</p></p>
