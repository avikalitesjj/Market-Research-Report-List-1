<p><strong>The Gauge Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Remote-Sealed Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gauge pressure transmitters market comprises two primary types: in-line and remote-sealed gauge pressure transmitters. In-line gauge pressure transmitters are directly installed within the process flow, providing real-time pressure measurements suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, remote-sealed gauge pressure transmitters feature a diaphragm that separates the sensing element from the process fluid, allowing for accurate pressure readings in applications with extreme temperatures or corrosive media. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring accurate pressure monitoring and control in various sectors.</p></p>
