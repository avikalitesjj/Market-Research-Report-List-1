<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Powder Ceramic Materials market is primarily divided into two types: α-SiC and β-SiC. α-SiC, or alpha silicon carbide, is known for its stability and high thermal conductivity, making it suitable for applications in high-temperature environments. In contrast, β-SiC, or beta silicon carbide, offers superior thermal resistance and is often used in electronic devices and semiconductor applications. Both types are critical for various industries, including automotive, electronics, and aerospace, due to their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
