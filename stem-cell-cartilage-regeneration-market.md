<p><p>The stem cell cartilage regeneration market comprises two main types: cell-based and non-cell-based approaches. </p><p>Cell-based approaches involve the use of stem cells, either harvested from the patient or sourced from donors, to help repair or regenerate damaged cartilage through differentiation and tissue engineering techniques. </p><p>Non-cell-based approaches focus on the use of biomaterials, growth factors, and scaffolding to support cartilage healing without involving the direct application of live cells.
