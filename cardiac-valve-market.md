<p><p>The cardiac valve market includes various types, each serving distinct needs. Trans-catheter heart valves are minimally invasive and suitable for high-risk patients. Tissue heart valves, made from animal or human tissue, offer natural function and reduce clot risk but may require replacement over time. Mechanical heart valves, made from durable materials, provide longevity but come with a higher risk of blood clots, necessitating lifelong anticoagulation. Additionally, the market encompasses other devices and innovations catering to specific patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
