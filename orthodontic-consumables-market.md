<p><p>The orthodontic consumables market primarily comprises ligatures, brackets, and archwires, essential for dental correction. Ligatures are small elastics or wires that hold archwires in place on brackets. Brackets are attachments bonded to teeth, guiding movement through connected archwires. Archwires are flexible wires that provide the force necessary to align teeth. These components work collaboratively to facilitate effective orthodontic treatment, allowing for precise tooth movement and improved dental aesthetics, ultimately enhancing patient satisfaction and oral health outcomes.</p></p>
