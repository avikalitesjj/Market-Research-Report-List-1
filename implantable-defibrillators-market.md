<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Implantable Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (S-ICDS)</li><li>Transvenous Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator (T-ICDs)</li><li>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator (CRT-D)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable defibrillator market includes three primary types: Subcutaneous Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (S-ICDs), which are implanted under the skin to monitor and treat life-threatening arrhythmias without lead placement in the heart; Transvenous Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators (T-ICDs), which use leads inserted into the heart for direct monitoring and shock delivery; and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-Ds), designed to treat heart failure by coordinating heart chamber contractions while also providing defibrillation.</p></p>
