<p><ul><li>Hardtail Suspension</li><li>Full Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike suspension market is primarily divided into two types: hardtail and full suspension. Hardtail bikes feature a solid rear frame with no rear suspension, providing efficiency and lightweight advantages, making them ideal for cross-country riding. In contrast, full suspension bikes incorporate both front and rear shock absorbers, offering enhanced comfort and control on rough terrains, making them suitable for downhill and trail riding. Each type serves different riding preferences and conditions, catering to a diverse range of mountain biking enthusiasts.</p></p>
