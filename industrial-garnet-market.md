<p><p>The industrial garnet market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Almandine garnet, known for its hardness, is primarily used in abrasive materials and waterjet cutting. Andradite garnet, prized for its brilliance, finds use in filtration and specialty abrasives. Grossular garnet has applications in ceramics and as a gemstone. Pyrope garnet is utilized for various abrasive purposes, while Spessartine is favored in gemology. Uvarovite, the rarest type, is mostly used in niche markets and as a gemstone.</p></p>
