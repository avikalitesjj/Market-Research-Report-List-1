<p><strong>The Automated DNA Sequencers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing</li><li>Next Generation DNA Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated DNA Sequencers market comprises two main types: Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing and Next Generation DNA Sequencing (NGS). Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing, also known as Sanger sequencing, is a method that relies on selectively incorporating chain-terminating nucleotides during DNA replication, producing readable fragments. In contrast, NGS allows for massive parallel sequencing of millions of DNA fragments simultaneously, vastly increasing throughput and speed. Both methods are essential for genetic research, diagnostics, and biotechnology applications, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>
