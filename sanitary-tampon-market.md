<p><p>The sanitary tampon market comprises two primary types: conduit type and ductless tampons. Conduit type sanitary tampons feature an applicator that provides a guided insertion, ensuring ease and hygiene. This design appeals to users seeking convenience. In contrast, ductless tampons eliminate the applicator, offering a more compact and eco-friendly option, favored for portability and reduced waste. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, focusing on functionality, comfort, and sustainability in feminine hygiene products.</p></p>
