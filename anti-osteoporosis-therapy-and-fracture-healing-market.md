<p><p>Anti-Osteoporosis therapy encompasses various treatments aimed at preventing bone loss and promoting fracture healing. Bisphosphonates inhibit bone resorption, enhancing bone density. Calcitonin helps regulate calcium levels and reduces bone loss. Estrogen or hormone replacement therapy supports bone health, particularly in postmenopausal women. Biologics, such as denosumab, target specific pathways in bone metabolism to decrease resorption. Anabolic agents like teriparatide stimulate bone formation, promoting healing. These therapies collectively address osteoporosis and improve patient outcomes in managing bone health.</p></p>
