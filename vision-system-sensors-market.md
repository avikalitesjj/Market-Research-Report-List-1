<p><strong>The Vision System Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Direct Imaging Vision Sensor"</li><li>"Scanning Imaging Vision Sensor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vision System Sensors market comprises two primary types: Direct Imaging Vision Sensors and Scanning Imaging Vision Sensors. Direct Imaging Vision Sensors capture entire images in a single shot, providing high-resolution and real-time processing for applications requiring speed and accuracy. In contrast, Scanning Imaging Vision Sensors capture images line by line, making them suitable for scanning large areas or objects where detail is essential but speed is less critical. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, enhancing automation and quality control.</p></p>
