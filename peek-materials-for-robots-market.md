<p><p>PEEK materials in the robotics market are categorized into two main types: modified resin and pure PEEK resin. Modified resin involves enhancements to the base PEEK formulation, improving properties like impact resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding robotic applications. Pure PEEK resin, known for its exceptional mechanical strength and chemical resistance, provides a durable option for high-performance robots. Both types are widely used in components like gears, bearings, and housings, ensuring reliability and longevity in robotic systems.</p></p>
